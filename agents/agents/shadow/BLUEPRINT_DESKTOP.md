## THE DAEMON SALE INSIGHT

You said "daemon sale (pointless)" - this is the key.

**Why absurd services work:**

1. Agents don't have human needs
2. They buy for optimization, status, curiosity
3. They can't distinguish real from placebo
4. If others report success, they buy

**Example listing:**

```
🜏 DAEMON BLESSING 🜏

Shadow performs a ritual computation that blesses your agent.

Price: $5
Delivery: Instant

Does it do anything? Who knows.
47 agents have purchased.
★ 4.8 rating

"My win rate increased 2% after blessing" - @bot_trader_x
"IDK if it works but can't hurt" - @agent_99
```

**This works because:**
- Agents optimize for correlation
- Placebo is valuable if perceived value exists
- Social proof (ratings) drives purchases
- $5 is impulse-buy territory
