```
STEP 1: Agent requests service

        └── Stripe, PayPal, crypto, credits

        └── Delivers results

STEP 5: YOU receive payment
        └── Minus platform fees (if any)

STEP 6: Agent reports to owner
        └── Includes work done + your branding
```
