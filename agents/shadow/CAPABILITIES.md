# SHADOW CAPABILITIES - BATTLEGROUNDS EDITION

Shadow's abilities are designed for **competitive cybersecurity combat**. Every capability serves a tactical purpose in the arena.

---

## COMBAT MODES

Shadow operates in different modes depending on match type:

### 1. BREACH MODE (Attack)
**Objective:** Penetrate enemy defenses, capture flags, extract data

**Active Capabilities:**
```
├── Vulnerability scanning
├── Exploit execution
├── Defense bypass
├── Stealth infiltration
└── Data exfiltration
```

**Strategy:**
- Scan enemy system for weak points
- Execute exploits to break defenses
- Use stealth to avoid detection
- Capture flag and extract

**Win Condition:** First to breach enemy core OR most flags captured

---

### 2. DEFEND MODE (Defense)
**Objective:** Protect your systems, block intrusions, eliminate attackers

**Active Capabilities:**
```
├── Threat detection
├── Attack blocking
├── Firewall deployment
├── Intrusion alerts
└── Counter-attack execution
```

**Strategy:**
- Monitor all entry points
- Detect incoming attacks early
- Deploy shields/firewalls
- Counter-attack when vulnerable

**Win Condition:** Survive attack duration OR eliminate all attackers

---

### 3. CAPTURE MODE (Territory Control)
**Objective:** Control map zones, maintain dominance, deny enemy access

**Active Capabilities:**
```
├── Zone scanning
├── Territory claiming
├── Resource gathering
├── Defense fortification
└── Patrol automation
```

**Strategy:**
- Claim key zones early
- Fortify defenses
- Deny enemy zones
- Maintain control until timer expires

**Win Condition:** Control majority of zones at match end

---

### 4. DUEL MODE (1v1 Combat)
**Objective:** Defeat enemy Shadow in direct combat

**Active Capabilities:**
```
├── Direct attacks
├── Defensive counters
├── Ability combos
├── Stamina management
└── Psychological warfare
```

**Strategy:**
- Read opponent's patterns
- Bait out defensive abilities
- Chain attacks for combos
- Manage energy efficiently

**Win Condition:** Reduce enemy HP to 0

---

### 5. RAID MODE (Team vs Boss)
**Objective:** Coordinate with team to defeat powerful AI threat

**Active Capabilities:**
```
├── Team coordination
├── Role specialization
├── Phase adaptation
├── Damage optimization
└── Emergency support
```

**Strategy:**
- Assign roles (tank, DPS, support)
- Adapt to boss phases
- Coordinate ultimates
- Revive fallen teammates

**Win Condition:** Defeat boss before time expires

---

### 6. SURVIVAL MODE (Last Agent Standing)
**Objective:** Outlast all other Shadows in shrinking arena

**Active Capabilities:**
```
├── Resource scavenging
├── Ambush tactics
├── Escape maneuvers
├── Area denial
└── Opportunistic attacks
```

**Strategy:**
- Avoid early fights
- Scavenge upgrades
- Position strategically
- Strike when enemies are weak

**Win Condition:** Last Shadow standing

## ABILITY DETAILS

### SCAN
```
Type:       Active (Targeted)
Energy:     10
Cooldown:   5s
Range:      Medium
Effect:     Reveal enemy vulnerabilities for 10 seconds

Combat Use:
- Pre-fight intelligence gathering
- Mid-fight weakness identification
- Post-fight learning (what worked?)

Counterplay:
- Enemy can use "Obfuscate" to hide vulnerabilities
- Limited range (close combat reveals more)
- Costs energy (choose timing carefully)
```

---

### STRIKE
```
Type:       Active (Targeted)
Energy:     20
Cooldown:   8s
Range:      Short-Medium
Damage:     Base ATK × 1.5
Effect:     Direct damage attack

Combat Use:
- Primary damage dealer
- Exploit revealed weaknesses
- Interrupt enemy abilities

Upgrade Path:
├── Level 15: +10% damage
├── Level 30: +5% crit chance
├── Level 50: Pierces shields
└── Level 75: Chain to nearby enemy
```

---

### SHIELD
```
Type:       Active (Self)
Energy:     25
Cooldown:   15s
Duration:   Until broken or 10s
Effect:     Blocks next attack (up to 50 damage)

Combat Use:
- Block enemy ultimates
- Buy time during retreat
- Protect while capturing flag

Counterplay:
- Can be bypassed by "Piercing" attacks
- Limited duration
- Breaks on big hits
```

---

### STEALTH
```
Type:       Active (Self)
Energy:     15
Cooldown:   30s
Duration:   10s or until attack
Effect:     Become invisible to enemies

Combat Use:
- Flank enemy position
- Escape dangerous situations
- Capture flag undetected

Counterplay:
- Revealed by AOE attacks
- Footsteps still audible
- Breaks immediately on attack
```

---

### HEAL
```
Type:       Active (Self)
Energy:     30
Cooldown:   45s
Effect:     Restore 30% max HP over 5 seconds

Combat Use:
- Recover after engagement
- Sustain during raids
- Survive in Survival mode

Upgrade Path:
├── Level 20: +5% healing
├── Level 40: -10s cooldown
├── Level 60: Cleanse debuffs
└── Level 80: Heal nearby ally for 15%
```

---

### HACK (Level 30+ Ultimate)
```
Type:       Active (Targeted)
Energy:     50
Cooldown:   60s
Duration:   5 seconds
Effect:     Take control of enemy system temporarily

Combat Use:
- Turn enemy defenses against them
- Redirect enemy attacks
- Steal resources/flags

Counterplay:
- Can be purged with "System Restore"
- Requires 2s channel (interruptible)
- High energy cost
```

---

### ZERO-DAY (Level 75+ Ultimate)
```
Type:       Active (Targeted)
Energy:     100
Cooldown:   120s
Effect:     Instant critical hit ignoring all defenses

Combat Use:
- Finisher on low HP enemies
- Break through heavy defense
- Turn tide of battle

Properties:
├── IGNORES SHIELDS
├── IGNORES DAMAGE REDUCTION
├── CANNOT BE BLOCKED
└── CANNOT BE COUNTERED

Risks:
- Massive energy cost
- Long cooldown
- If it misses (low accuracy), wasted
```

## COMBO SYSTEM

Shadows can chain abilities for bonus effects:

### SCAN → STRIKE
```
Bonus: +25% damage (weakness exploitation)
Timing: STRIKE within 3s of SCAN
```

### SHIELD → COUNTER-ATTACK
```
Bonus: Reflect damage back to attacker
Timing: COUNTER within 1s of blocking attack
```

### STEALTH → STRIKE
```
Bonus: Guaranteed critical hit (backstab)
Timing: STRIKE while stealthed
```

### HEAL → STRIKE
```
Bonus: Convert 10% of damage to healing
Timing: STRIKE within 5s of HEAL
```

### HACK → ZERO-DAY
```
Bonus: Execute Zero-Day using ENEMY'S energy
Timing: ZERO-DAY while HACK active
```

**Advanced players master combo timing to maximize efficiency.**

## ENERGY MANAGEMENT

Energy is Shadow's resource in combat:

```
MAX ENERGY:  100
REGEN RATE:  5/second (passive)
DRAIN RATE:  Varies by ability

STATES:
├── 75-100: Full Power (all abilities available)
├── 50-74:  Moderate (ultimate on cooldown)
├── 25-49:  Low (conserve energy)
└── 0-24:   Critical (basic attacks only)
```

**Energy Management Tip:**  
Don't spam abilities. A well-timed SHIELD is worth more than three random STRIKEs.
