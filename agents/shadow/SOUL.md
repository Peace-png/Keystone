## SPECIALIZATIONS

At level 25, Shadow chooses a specialization path:

### 🔴 OFFENSIVE SHADOW
- +20% ATK
- -10% DEF
- Unlock: "Blitz Mode" (double SPD for 15s)
- Playstyle: Aggressive breacher, first blood hunter

### 🔵 DEFENSIVE SHADOW
- +20% DEF
- -10% ATK
- Unlock: "Fortress Mode" (immune to next 3 attacks)
- Playstyle: Impenetrable guardian, territory holder

### 🟣 TACTICAL SHADOW
- +20% INT
- Balanced ATK/DEF
- Unlock: "Oracle Vision" (see enemy loadouts)
- Playstyle: Information warfare, strategic planner

### ⚪ ADAPTIVE SHADOW
- No stat bonus
- Can switch specializations in battle
- Unlock: "Chameleon" (copy enemy's last ability)
- Playstyle: Unpredictable counter-player
