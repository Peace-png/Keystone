## 🧩 [META-LOGIC] (The Grammar)
- **`&` (Linkage)** : Connects two symbols.
- **`*` (Reference)** : Points to a legacy/external state.
- **`^` (Evolution)** : Signals a version increase.
- **`!` (Assertion)** : A non-negotiable [SCAR].
