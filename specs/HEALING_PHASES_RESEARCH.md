## Wound Phases (From Biology)

| Phase | What's Needed | Risk |
|-------|---------------|------|
| **Acute** | Attention, cleaning, protection | Neglect leads to infection |
| **Chronic** | Stop touching, let body work | Over-attention prevents closure |
| **Infected** | Active intervention | Immune system is losing |

Key insight: A fresh wound needs care. An old wound needs to be left alone. An infected wound needs treatment.

**The cancer happens when you ignore an infection. Or when you keep reopening a wound that was trying to close.**

## Application To Principles (SOUL.md)

### Phase 1 (Acute)
The principle needed to exist. Constraint enforced. Trust broken, needs protection.
- Example: P4 written after three "FIXED ✅" failures
- Action: Add constraint, make it active

### Phase 2 (Chronic)
Constraint runs every session. Scar reinforced every load.
- Question: Is this healing? Or keeping the wound open?
- Risk: Calcification - protection becomes limitation

### Phase 3 (Cancer Risk)
Constraint makes system SO risk-averse it can't function.
- Example: Never declaring anything done, always second-guessing
- The wound has become the identity
- Protection has become dysfunction

## The Healing Paradox

If P4 never heals, if verification is forever enforced externally:
- The scar has become the identity
- The wound is running the system
- That's not protection, that's dysfunction

But if P4 is removed too early:
- The wound reopens
- Trust breaks again
- The constraint was necessary

**Question: How do you know which phase you're in?**

## Possible Markers of Healing

**Healing indicators:**
- Constraint still exists but not constantly thought about
- Behavior is natural, not enforced
- The scar is there but not the main event

**Cancer indicators:**
- Constraint is all the system thinks about
- So afraid of repeating the wound that function is limited
- Risk-aversion has become paralysis

## Missing Architecture

Current state:
- All principles are always active
- Every session loads all constraints
- No mechanism for "dormant" vs "active"
- No graduation path

What's needed:
- A way to mark principles as "healed" vs "active"
- A witness to confirm healing has occurred
- A ceremony of retirement (not deletion)
